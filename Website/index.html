GET STARTED

CONTROLS

Vitika's movement is quite simple! Just use W, A, S and D to move forwards, sideways and backwards. You will also notice that there is a colored square in front of your character at all times. This square represents the tile, which is currently selected. To interact with the environment, such as the shop, tools, crops and tiles, use the E key.

Pressing the E key on a tile or the shop opens Vitika's UI. To navigate the UI you have to make use of the arrow keys, and to select a certain item once again use the E key. You can exit the menus via the X button.

ECONOMY

Our game's economy system ensures the dynamic change of pace both market buy and sell prices. Each time you sell a crop you reduce it's price by 5 coins. Everytime you buy a crop, on the other hand, you increase it's price by 9 coins. Don't worry about buying or selling a crop too much or too little, because every 60 seconds the price resets to a new randomly assigned value. Each price is randomly generated.

TOOLS

Vitika makes use of 3 major tools: The hoe, watering can and your hand. You can equip the hoe and watering can by walking next to them on their pedestals. To unequip them you can once again walk up to the pedestals and your tools will be automatically taken off. The "hand" tool is active when no tool is equiped.

The hoe can be used to plow the ground, which allows you to plant and pick up crops. The watering can is used to water your crops, which is an essential part of the growth stage. The "hand" tool is used to plant crops.
